# Product decisions

## Which features did you include?

I included only new employee's features.

- Sign-in and register
- Having a dashboard with general information
- Seeing and managing assigned tasks for an employee

## How did you prioritize them?

I chose the features that show the real value of the platform in the learning process of a new person in the company. I chose to implement a simple method of authentication to simulate the real userflow for a employee accessing the platform. Considering the timeframe I chose to focus on a couple of features, so that I can develop them in a clean and organised manner.

## Which features did you intentionally leave out of scope?

I intentionally left out the functionalities meant for the HR because they help in managing the platform information, but don't show the impact on the primary users.
